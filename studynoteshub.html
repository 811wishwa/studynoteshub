About StudyNotes Hub

StudyNotes Hub is a modern platform designed to help students share and access study materials easily. Instead of searching through multiple WhatsApp groups, emails, and scattered folders, students can find everything they need in one place.

Our platform features a sleek dark theme with modern green gradient accents, creating a comfortable viewing experience while maintaining a professional academic aesthetic.

Key Features:

  • Browse notes by subject categories
  • Search functionality to find specific materials
  • Upload and share your own study notes
  • Rating system to identify quality content
  • Fully responsive design for all devices
